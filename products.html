Regular E-10, dyed diesel, and road diesel are all available by delivery with a 200 gal minimum purchase.

Fertilizer

Want to grow a bumper crop? Everyone knows that rain is a big factor in making that happen, but certainly isn't the only one. If your crop can get thirsty, can't it also get hungry? The truth is, one of the best ways to maximize your yield is to make sure that it has a strong food supply. And in order for the soil to feed the crop, you have to feed the soil. At Service and Supply, we offer a wide selection of fertilizers so that you can get exactly what you need to get your bumper crop fed.
